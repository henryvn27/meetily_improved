# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Meetily Improved is a desktop workspace for solo founders who spend their day moving between calls, decisions, and follow-up. They need to begin a recording quickly, trust that the meeting stays local, read useful notes immediately afterward, and ask questions across saved meetings without maintaining another cloud system.

The primary workflow is not “manage meeting software.” It is capture a real conversation, return to the resulting knowledge, and continue working with minimal ceremony.

## Product Purpose

Meetily Improved is an open-source, local-first replacement for cloud meeting-note tools. It preserves Meetily’s recording, transcription, summary, import, recovery, and settings behavior while making the entire experience feel like one coherent desktop product.

Success means a founder can understand the current state within seconds, complete the primary task without hunting through utility panels, and trust every claim because the interface only presents data and progress backed by local application state.

## Brand Personality

Focused, calm, exact.

The product should feel quietly capable rather than decorative. It should have the confidence and density of a serious tool, the reading comfort of a well-edited document, and enough distinctive character to be recognizable without competing with the meeting itself.

## Anti-references

- Upstream Meetily’s collection of utility screens, mixed component styles, hard-coded gray/blue controls, and form-first visual hierarchy.
- The previous warm cream, rounded-card dashboard system, which still looked like a themed version of upstream rather than a new product.
- Generic AI dashboards with oversized greetings, equal-weight card grids, decorative gradients, fake metrics, fake progress, and invented assistant states.
- Literal copies of Granola, Codex, or Scoutly assets, layouts, protected copy, iconography, or proprietary interaction patterns.
- Cloud-first meeting products that hide local storage, recovery, model state, or errors behind optimistic copy.

## Design Principles

1. **The tool disappears behind the work.** Navigation and controls remain predictable and compact; transcript, summary, and decisions own the visual hierarchy.
2. **Local truth is visible.** Every status, date, result, citation, and progress treatment must come from an authoritative local contract. Missing data is explained, never invented.
3. **One screen, one priority.** Each route has a clear primary task. Secondary configuration and utilities remain available without competing for attention.
4. **Behavior survives visual change.** Redesign work must preserve recording, import, recovery, editing, playback, summary, chat, settings, and accessibility contracts.
5. **Original synthesis, not imitation.** Scoutly informs rigor and rhythm, Codex informs tool clarity, and Granola informs reading hierarchy. Meetily Improved uses its own restrained signal-orange identity and remains visually and legally distinct from all three.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG 2.2 AA contrast and interaction requirements. Every action must be keyboard reachable with a visible focus treatment. State may never rely on color alone. Respect reduced motion, preserve usable layouts at the supported desktop minimum size, use plain-language errors, and keep long transcripts readable at user-selected zoom and text sizes.
